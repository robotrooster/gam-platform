title: Finding your way around the landlord portal
---
The GAM landlord portal groups its navigation into these sections. These are the actual section names:

**Overview**
- **Dashboard** — your home overview when you log in.
- **Support** — chat with the GAM support team (where you are now).

**Portfolio**
- **Properties** — your properties.
- **Unit Overview** — your units and their status.
- **Master Schedule** — the booking/occupancy calendar for units.
- **Bookings** — short-term and scheduled unit bookings.
- **Tenants** — your tenants.
- **Tenant Onboarding** — moving a new tenant through setup.
- **Leases** / **Subleases** — lease agreements and any subleases.
- **E-Sign** — documents out for signature.

**Financials**
- **Disbursements** — what's paid out to you and how it's split.
- **Banking** — your connected bank and identity verification.
- **Payments** — rent and other payments coming in.
- **Reports** — reporting across your portfolio.
- **Agent Activity** — a log of the support-agent conversations on your account.

**Operations**
- **Maintenance** — maintenance requests from tenants, triage, and approvals.
- **Inspections** — move-in, move-out, and periodic inspections.
- **Entry Requests** — requests to enter a unit, with reason and notice.
- **Documents** — documents tied to your properties and tenancies.
- **Point of Sale** / **Inventory** — the POS surface for in-person charges and the items you track.
- **Work Trade** — work-trade arrangements with tenants.

**Screening**
- **Applicant Pool** / **Background Checks** — applicants and their screening/background results.
- **Tenant Record** / **Record Event** — a tenant's record and logging an event to it.

**Admin**
- **Team** — your team members and their access.
- **PM Invitations** — invitations to/from property-management companies.
- **Settings** — account and portfolio settings.
- **Notification Prefs** — how you're notified.

If you can't find something or aren't sure which section to use, tell the support team what you're trying to do and they'll point you to the right place — or handle it directly. Don't guess at a section name; only the ones above exist.
