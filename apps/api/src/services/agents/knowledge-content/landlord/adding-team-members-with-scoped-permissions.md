title: Adding team members and giving them the right access
---
As your portfolio grows, you'll want help running it. GAM lets you invite team members and decide exactly what each one can see and do, so nobody has more access than they need.

You can add four kinds of team members:

- Property Manager — your right hand for day-to-day operations: properties, units, tenants, leases, and team management when you grant it.
- On-Site Manager — someone stationed at a property handling on-the-ground tasks.
- Maintenance — a worker who receives and works repair requests.
- Bookkeeper — read-only or read-write access to your books.

To add someone, open your Team page and send an invitation to their email address. They'll get a link to set up (or sign in to) their account and join your team. Invitation links expire after 24 hours for security — if one lapses, just resend it from the same page, which issues a fresh link. You can also revoke a pending invitation any time before it's accepted.

A few things worth knowing:

- One email, one role. If an address is already registered under a different role, that person can't accept a worker invite with it.
- An on-site manager can only work for one landlord across the whole platform, so that invite won't go through if they're already on-site for someone else.
- You're always in control. Team members can't widen their own access or change their own permissions — only you (or someone you've trusted with team management) can adjust their scope.

When you remove a team member, their access ends immediately. Start small, grant only what each person needs, and expand as you build trust.
