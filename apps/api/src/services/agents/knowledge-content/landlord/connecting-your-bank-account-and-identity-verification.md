title: Connecting Your Bank Account & Identity Verification
---
To receive payouts in GAM, you connect a bank account through Stripe Connect, our payment partner. This includes an identity verification step (often called KYC) that's required before any money can be paid out to you.

## What you'll do

1. Start Stripe Connect onboarding from your GAM account.
2. Provide the requested identity and business details so Stripe can verify you.
3. Add the bank account where you want payouts deposited.

Until onboarding and verification are complete, GAM can't send you payouts, even if tenants have already paid.

## Why verification is required

Identity verification is a standard requirement for moving money. It helps confirm you are who you say you are and that funds go to the right account. GAM is the platform handling this process — we are not your landlord and don't own your properties; we simply route your tenants' electronic payments to you.

## Account fees

Stripe charges a small monthly fee per Connect account. GAM absorbs this $1/month fee, and it's waived once you have 10 or more billable units — so connecting your account doesn't add a separate charge from us.

## If something goes wrong

If verification won't complete, your bank account is rejected, or you need to update where payouts go, anything touching account access, security, or moving money is escalated to a human GAM specialist who can help directly.
