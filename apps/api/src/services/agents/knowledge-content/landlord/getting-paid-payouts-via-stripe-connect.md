GAM is the platform that processes rent and other charges on your behalf — GAM is not your landlord and does not own your properties. When tenants pay through GAM, the money moves to you through Stripe Connect, our payment infrastructure partner.

## How payouts flow

1. A tenant pays electronically through GAM (bank account/ACH or card).
2. The funds clear through Stripe.
3. Once cleared, GAM sends a payout to the bank account you connected.

Payouts run on a GAM-managed schedule after tenant funds have cleared. Because cards and bank transfers take time to settle, there's a normal clearing window between when a tenant pays and when the money reaches your account.

## Before you can receive payouts

You must connect a bank account and complete identity verification (KYC) through Stripe Connect onboarding. Until that's finished, payouts can't be sent. See the article on connecting your bank account for the step-by-step.

## Important notes

- Payments in GAM are electronic only. If a tenant pays you in cash outside the platform, that charge stays marked "unpaid" in GAM until they pay electronically.
- You set the rent amount, due date, grace period, and late fees in each lease — GAM does not set these.

If you have a question about a specific payout, a missing or delayed transfer, or anything involving moving or refunding money, we'll connect you with a human GAM specialist.
