title: Using the in-app support assistant
---
The in-app support assistant is the built-in helper inside your GAM account. It's there to answer questions quickly, in plain language, without making you dig through menus or wait on a reply. You can open it from anywhere in the app and just type what you're wondering about.

It's good at account-aware questions, meaning it can look at your own account to give you a specific answer. Some things people ask it:

- Payments: "What do I owe right now?", "When is my next payment due?", "Why was there a fee on my last payment?" (Payments on GAM are electronic. ACH bank transfers carry a small fee, and card payments carry a slightly higher one; a per-property setting decides whether the tenant or the landlord covers it.)
- Lease: "Where do I find my signed lease?", "What's my lease end date?"
- Maintenance: "How do I file a maintenance request?", "What's the status of the one I sent?" (You file requests in GAM and they're routed to your landlord, who handles the actual repair.)
- Notifications: "How do I stop getting text alerts?" or "How do I turn email reminders back on?"

A couple of things to know. For anything that depends on your specific lease terms or local rules, the assistant will point you to your lease and let your landlord weigh in, since those calls aren't GAM's to make. And if your question needs a human, the assistant will help you hand off to the GAM support team so you're not stuck.

The more specific your question, the better the answer. "Why is my balance higher this month?" works better than "help." Give it a try, it's usually the quickest way to get unstuck.
