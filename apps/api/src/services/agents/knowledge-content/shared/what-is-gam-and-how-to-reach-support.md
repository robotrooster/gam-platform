title: What GAM is and how to reach a real person
---
GAM is the software platform that connects renters and landlords in one place. Think of it as the system that handles the day-to-day: paying rent electronically, filing maintenance requests, signing your lease, and keeping a clear record of it all. GAM is the platform, not your landlord. Your landlord sets the things specific to your home, like your rent amount, when it's due, any grace period, and late fees. GAM gives everyone the shared tools to manage it smoothly.

A few things GAM does behind the scenes: it sends you notifications when something needs your attention (a new invoice, a maintenance update, a message from your landlord), and it lets you choose how you hear about each kind of update. In your notification settings you can turn email, text message, and in-app alerts on or off per topic, so you only get pinged the way you want.

When you need help, you have options:

- In-app support assistant: the fastest way to get answers. Open it from anywhere in your account and ask about payments, your lease, maintenance, fees, or how something works. It can look at your account to give you a real answer, not a generic one.
- Human support: if the assistant can't resolve it, or you'd just rather talk to a person, you can reach the GAM support team. They handle platform and account questions.
- Your landlord: anything specific to your home, lease terms, deposits, or repairs gets routed to your landlord, since those decisions are theirs to make.

If you're ever unsure who to ask, start with the in-app assistant. It'll either answer or point you to the right place.
