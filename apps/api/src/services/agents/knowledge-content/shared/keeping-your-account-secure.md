---
Your GAM account holds sensitive information about your rental and your payments, so it's worth a few minutes to keep it secure.

## Everyday habits

- **Use a strong, unique password.** Don't reuse a password from another site, and make it long. A password manager makes this easy.
- **Keep your email secure.** Your email is the recovery path for your GAM account — if someone controls it, they can request password resets. Protect it with its own strong password.
- **Sign out on shared devices.** If you use a public or shared computer, log out when you're done.
- **Be cautious with links.** GAM will only send password reset links when you request one from the **Forgot Password** page. Be wary of unexpected messages asking for your password or login details.

## If something looks wrong

Contact us right away if you:

- Notice activity on your account you don't recognize
- Can no longer sign in or have lost access to your account email
- Think someone else may have your password

Account security and access issues are always escalated to a human GAM specialist, who can help you recover and secure your account safely. Don't keep retrying on your own if you're locked out — reach out and ask to be connected.

## A note on roles

GAM is the platform that secures your login and account. It is not your landlord and does not make decisions about your property or lease. Keeping your own credentials safe is the most important step in protecting your account.
