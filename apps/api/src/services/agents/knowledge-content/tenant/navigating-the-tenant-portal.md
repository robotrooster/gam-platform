title: Finding your way around the tenant portal
---
The GAM tenant portal is organized into these sections, which you reach from the navigation menu. These are the actual sections — if you're looking for something, it's in one of these:

- **Home** — your starting point when you log in, with an at-a-glance overview.
- **Lease** — your lease agreement: the terms, rent amount, dates, and policies. You can read it here and open or download a signed copy.
- **Application** — your rental application and background/credit-check status.
- **Flex Advantage** — optional Flex products your landlord may offer (such as flexible payment timing or deposit options). You'll only see this if your landlord has enabled it at your property.
- **Payments** — pay your rent, see your payment history, and manage the bank account or card you pay with.
- **Maintenance** — file a maintenance request and track the status of ones you've opened. Requests go to your landlord.
- **Support** — chat with the GAM support team (this is where you are now).
- **Inspections** — move-in, move-out, and any periodic inspections, with the details and results.
- **My walkthroughs** — the inspection walkthrough videos you've recorded for your unit.
- **Entry Requests** — requests from your landlord to enter your unit, showing the reason, the proposed time, and how much notice was given.
- **My Record** — your record as a renter on GAM.
- **My Disputes** — any disputes you've opened and where they stand.
- **Notifications** — your notifications, with a Preferences area where you choose how you're contacted (email, text, or in-app).
- **Payouts** — money on its way back to you, such as a deposit return.
- **Profile** — your account and profile settings.

If you can't find something or aren't sure which section to use, just tell the support team what you're trying to do and they'll point you to the right place — or handle it for you directly. There is no "Documents" item in the main menu: your lease lives under **Lease**, and a document waiting for your signature shows up under Lease and in your Notifications. (There is a Documents page where your signed documents are listed, but it isn't a menu item — the support team can point you to it.)
