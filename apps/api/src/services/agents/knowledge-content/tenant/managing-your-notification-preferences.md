title: Managing your notification preferences
---
GAM keeps you in the loop about your rental — payment reminders, maintenance updates, messages from your landlord, and more. You decide how each kind of notification reaches you, so you hear about what matters without the noise you don't want.

You'll find these controls in your notification preferences. They're organized by type of notification, and for each type you can turn three delivery channels on or off independently:

- Email — sent to the address on your account.
- SMS — text messages to your phone.
- In-app — the alerts you see inside GAM, with an unread count so nothing slips by.

Because the channels are separate per type, you can fine-tune things to your liking. For example, you might keep email and in-app on for payment reminders but skip the texts, while leaving SMS on for time-sensitive maintenance updates. Flip whichever toggles fit how you prefer to be reached; your choices save right away and apply going forward.

Inside the app, your notifications collect in one place. You can mark a single notification as read, or clear everything at once with a "mark all as read" action — handy when the unread count has piled up. Marking something read just tidies your list; it doesn't delete the underlying update.

A couple of things worth knowing: keep your email address and phone number accurate so messages actually reach you on those channels. And some essential account messages — like a password reset you requested — are always delivered regardless of your preferences, because they're tied to keeping your account secure.

GAM is the platform behind your rental account, not your landlord, but these preferences are how we make sure the right updates find you the right way.
