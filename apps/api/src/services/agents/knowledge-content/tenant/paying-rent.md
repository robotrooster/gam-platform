title: Paying Rent: Bank Account vs. Card
---
GAM is an electronic-only payment platform, so all rent and other charges are paid online — there is no option to pay by cash or check inside GAM. If you pay your landlord in cash outside the platform, that payment will still show as unpaid in GAM until you make an electronic payment here.

You can pay two ways:

- **Bank account (ACH):** A transfer directly from your checking or savings account.
- **Card:** A debit or credit card.

## What it costs to pay

A processing fee may apply depending on how you pay:

- **ACH (bank account):** 1.0% of the payment, capped at $6.00.
- **Card:** 3.25% of the payment.
- **Canadian cards charged in USD:** an additional 1.5%.

Because ACH is capped at $6.00, paying from a bank account is usually the lowest-cost option for larger payments.

## Who pays the fee

Whether you or your landlord covers this processing fee is set per property. Your payment screen shows any fee before you confirm, so you'll always see the total before you pay.

A note on roles: GAM is the platform that moves your payment — it is not your landlord and does not set your rent. The rent amount, due date, and any late fees come from your lease with your landlord.

Have a question about a specific charge or a payment that didn't go through? We can help, and anything involving moving or refunding money is handed to a human GAM specialist.
