title: Resetting your password and keeping your account secure
---
Forgot your password, or just want to refresh it? You can reset it yourself in a few steps, and a little routine care keeps your GAM account safe.

To reset a forgotten password, head to the sign-in screen and choose the "Forgot password" option. Enter the email address tied to your account and we'll send a reset link to your inbox. Open that email, follow the link, and choose a new password. The link is time-sensitive for your protection, so if it expires before you use it, just request a fresh one.

If you're already signed in and simply want to change your password, you can update it from your account settings. You don't need the email link in that case — just confirm your current password and set the new one.

A few habits that go a long way:

- Pick a password you don't reuse anywhere else, and make it long rather than clever. A passphrase of a few unrelated words is both stronger and easier to remember.
- Keep the email address on your account current. That inbox is how you recover access and how we reach you about anything important, so it's worth getting right.
- Sign out when you're on a shared or public device.
- If a password-reset email arrives that you didn't request, you can safely ignore it — nothing changes until someone actually completes the reset. If those keep coming, change your password as a precaution.

GAM is the software platform that powers your rental account; we're not your landlord. If something looks off with your account and you can't sort it out from your settings, reach out to support and we'll help you get back in.
