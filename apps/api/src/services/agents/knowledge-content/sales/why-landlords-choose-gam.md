title: Why landlords choose GAM
---
GAM puts everything you need to run your rentals in one place — collecting rent, leases and e-signing, maintenance, tenant screening, and a clear view of your whole portfolio — instead of scattering it across spreadsheets, email, and a half-dozen apps. What sets GAM apart is that it's built from the ground up for the kinds of properties most tools treat as an afterthought: **RV parks, extended-stay, and nightly/weekly stays**, right alongside traditional apartments and single-family rentals.

Here's what landlords get:

- **Electronic rent, paid straight to your bank.** Tenants pay by bank transfer (ACH) or card — no chasing checks or waiting on mailed payments. As their payments clear, your payouts reach your connected bank account through Stripe on a regular schedule.
- **First-class support for RV parks & extended-stay.** Bookings, check-in/check-out, and flexible nightly / weekly / long-term stay types are built in — not bolted on.
- **Leases and e-signing built in.** Create a lease, send it for signature, and keep the signed copy on file — all in the portal.
- **Maintenance that routes itself.** Tenants file requests right in their portal; they come straight to you, with an approval step for bigger jobs so nothing happens without your sign-off.
- **Tenant screening.** Run applications and background checks on prospective tenants without leaving the platform.
- **A clear picture of your portfolio.** Occupancy, who's behind on rent, vacancies, lease expirations, and payouts — all at a glance.

GAM works for landlords of all sizes, scales as you grow, and is designed for operators anywhere in the country.
