title: What GAM costs
---
GAM's pricing is straightforward and built so you only pay for what you're actually using.

**Platform fee.** It's a small per-occupied-unit monthly fee — and **vacant units are never charged**, so you're not paying for empty space. There's a modest per-property monthly minimum as well. The more occupied your units, the more value you're getting; the fee scales with that, not against you.

**Tenant payment processing.** When tenants pay electronically, there's a processing fee — lower for bank (ACH) payments, a bit higher for cards. A per-property setting lets you decide whether the tenant covers that fee or you do; GAM never keeps your rent or absorbs these costs in a way that's hidden from you. You'll always see the breakdown.

**Getting paid.** Payouts to your bank run through Stripe, and the small per-account cost Stripe charges for that is absorbed into the platform fee — there's no separate line item for it.

For exact numbers tailored to your portfolio size and mix, the best path is a quick call with our team — they can lay out what it would look like for you specifically. I'm happy to set that up.
