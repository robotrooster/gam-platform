How it works

Rent in. Payouts out.
All on one rail.

Tenants pay through the platform; the money routes to your connected bank account via Stripe. No GAM intermediary sitting on your funds — you're paid as payments settle.

💳
Tenants pay by ACH or card
Every rent payment runs through Stripe. ACH or card, autopay or one-off — tenants get a clean portal, you get a single ledger.
🏦
Paid straight to your bank
Stripe Connect routes settled rent directly to your bank account — typically 1–2 business days after a payment clears. GAM never holds your money in between.
🔒
Eviction Mode built in
Accepting any rent can waive your eviction rights in many states. Eviction Mode hard-blocks all tenant payments the moment you flip it — preserving your position. (Check your local laws.)
📋
NACHA-compliant ACH
Built to the NACHA Phase 2 fraud-monitoring requirements (effective June 22, 2026) before we collect the first ACH — enforcement on R05, R07, R10, R29 return codes.
